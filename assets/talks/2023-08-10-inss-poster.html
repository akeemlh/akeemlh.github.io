Data Preservation at MINERνA: Saving muon fuzz

Fermilab, Illinois, United States
10th August 2023

This poster was presented at the 14th International Neutrino Summer School hosted at Fermilab in August 2023. It describes my contributions to data preservation efforts at MINERvA.
